Process and Planning

Learning how to interact with potential new clients is just as important as learning the technical skills to deliver the final product. This part of training focused on the business logistics aspect.

  1. Who are the actors?

  2. What are the features?

  3. List the requirements

  4. Estimate a quote

  5. Establish a proposal or contract

  6. Finalize quote and proposal

  7. Deliver in iterations
